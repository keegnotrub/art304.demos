Tapenade

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it’s always a big hit at parities. My father recommends:

Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve it. In the winder, try serving it warm.

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes & garlic in a food processor. Blend until as small as possible.
  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional).
